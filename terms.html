2. What FibreOps Is

FibreOps is a cloud-based field operations management platform designed for South African Internet Service Providers (ISPs) and fibre network operators. The platform enables organisations to:

  • Create, assign and track field jobs in real time
  • Manage field technicians and operations teams
  • Monitor SLA compliance per job
  • Record job completions with photos and documentation
  • Generate operational reports
